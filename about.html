About me



    Hi I'm Sethmi Gurusinghe from Sri Lanka ! I'm a line artist,
I wield the humble pen like a sorcerer's wand.I dance with colors,
whisper to canvases, and breathe life into imagination. My canvas
is a blank expanse of possibilities, waiting for me to breathe life
into it. Let me take you on a tour through my creative universe.
Welcome to my monochromatic universe.

    My Ink Trails

  • 🌟 Medium: Ink, watercolors, and digital enchantments.
    I dance between fine liners, quills, and digital styluses.
    Each stroke is a heartbeat, a whispered secret.


  • 🌿 Inspiration: Nature’s contours—the curve of a leaf, the jagged edge of a mountain range.
    Ancient manuscripts, forgotten symbols, and the rhythm of forgotten languages.


  • 🌑 Shadows & Light: My lines are shadows seeking light.
    I explore chiaroscuro—the dance of darkness and illumination.
    In the void, I find stories waiting to be etched


  • 🌈 Color Obsession: Turquoise—the color of secrets whispered by the sea.
    Crimson—the heartbeat of passion. And that elusive shade between dusk and dawn.


    Artistic Philosophy

  • 🌌 Minimalism: I believe that simplicity holds cosmic power.
    A single line can evoke an entire universe.
    My art is a hymn to the void, where less becomes more.


  • 🔮 Whispers of Emotion: My lines carry emotions—quiet grief, fierce joy, and the melancholy of forgotten dreams.
    I invite you to listen closely; you might hear echoes of your own heart


  • 🌊 Fluidity & Rhythm: My lines flow like rivers, guided by intuition. They twist, merge, and diverge.
    Each stroke is a heartbeat, a ripple in the cosmic pond.
